# Reviewer Checklist

- Is legacy output reproduced within the declared tolerance?
- Are representation, host count and replay held fixed in mechanism ablations?
- Are fusion decisions exported per sample or aggregate with reason codes?
- Is final-test data excluded from training, repair and selection?
- Are parameter, memory and latency deltas reported?
- Are overlapping contexts evaluated with soft targets?
- Are old-context gains accompanied by recent-task non-regression?
- Are SplitCIFAR10 and MNIST-family regression gates passed before promotion?
